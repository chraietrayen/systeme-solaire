Earth

Earth, the third planet from the Sun, is the only known planet to support life. It boasts a diverse climate and a variety of landforms, including majestic mountains, vast forests, and expansive oceans. Earth's atmosphere is composed of 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and trace amounts of other gases, creating a habitable environment for a wide array of life forms. The planet's unique water cycle and its axial tilt contribute to its seasons and climatic variations.

  • Diameter: 12,742 km (7,918 miles)
  • Distance from the Sun: 149.6 million km (93 million miles)
  • Orbital Period: 365.25 Earth days
  • Number of Moons: 1 (The Moon)
  • Atmospheric Composition: 78% Nitrogen, 21% Oxygen, 1% Other Gases
  • Surface Composition: 71% Water, 29% Land
  • Unique Feature: Only planet known to support life and have liquid water on its surface
