Jupiter

Jupiter, the giant of our solar system, is known for its Great Red Spot, a colossal storm that has been raging for centuries. This gas giant has an atmosphere primarily composed of hydrogen and helium, and an extremely powerful magnetic field. Jupiter is also distinguished by its spectacular polar auroras and the diversity of its moons, totaling 79, including the four large Galilean moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
