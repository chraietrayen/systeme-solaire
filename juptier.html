Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, known for its Great Red Spot, a giant storm that has been raging for centuries. It is a gas giant with an atmosphere composed mainly of hydrogen and helium. Jupiter has a strong magnetic field and at least 79 moons, including the four large Galilean moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.

  • Diameter: 139,820 km
  • Distance from Sun: 778.5 million km
  • Orbital Period: 12 years
  • Moons: 79
