Mars

Mars, the fourth planet from the Sun, is often called the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance. Mars has a thin atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide, and features surface characteristics of both deserts and polar ice caps. The planet is home to the tallest volcano and the deepest canyon in the solar system.

  • Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Distance from the Sun: 227.9 million km
  • Orbital Period: 687 Earth days
  • Number of Moons: 2 (Phobos and Deimos)
  • Tallest Volcano: Olympus Mons (21,287 meters)
  • Deepest Canyon: Valles Marineris (over 4,000 km long and up to 7 km deep)
