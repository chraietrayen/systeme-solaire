Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often referred to as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance. Mars has a thin atmosphere composed mostly of carbon dioxide, and it has surface features both of a dry desert and polar ice caps. Mars is home to the tallest volcano and the deepest canyon in the solar system.

  • Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Distance from Sun: 227.9 million km
  • Orbital Period: 687 days
  • Moons: 2 (Phobos and Deimos)
